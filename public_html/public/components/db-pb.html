Profile Buddy

Purpose

Profile buddy was designed to make getting your own university data more accessible. Often times students misremember what their own credentials are, and profile buddy deals with that concern. This is done by pulling up personal university data with the tap of the widget. Students can use this data as a reminder when filling out forms, or as a way to double check what they already know. In profile buddy, the user may also sign out of their account in case they need to switch to another university account or sign in again.

User Data

Data displayed in profile buddy includes:
  • An optional image they can upload by tapping the buddy
  • Their full name
  • A NetId, or the start of their email address and Dorm Buddy username
  • G-Number, or unique university identifying number
  • The building they reside in
  • Their dorm room number
