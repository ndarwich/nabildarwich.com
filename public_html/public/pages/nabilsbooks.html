Nabil's Books

Introduction
Nabil's Books is an online bookstore that serves books to millions of knowledge seekers worldwide. The knowledge it serves comes in a variety of file formats and sizes, and a vast number of requests get processed concurrently by the bookstore. Over time, as its business model is to serve all types of knowledge for free regardless of location, Nabil's Books has gained enough popularity to become an world renowned. Due to its popularity, however, consumer traffic has started causing longer than expected download speeds, and many customers leave unsatisfied with the free service due to that. The founder of the bookstore, Nabil, wants to keep his customer base happy, so he launches an investigation to find out the root cause of these latencies and how to address them.

The Bookstore
Knowledge comes in many forms, and many file formats as well. Nabil's Books is proud to serve books of common media formats, as well as wildcard file formats like .zip and .pptx. The following is a list of books served by Nabil's Books:
Text Books
The original media type that Nabil's Books serves is Text Books. Nabil's Text Books contain a vast mountain of information, all written in Latin. To exemplify how much knowledge Nabil's Text Books have, the smallest Text Book, 5MB.txt, has 757,760 words. The average reading speed is 200 words per minute, so it would take an average person 63 hours to read that book alone.
Image Books
Sometimes, knowledge can be captured through images instead of text, and that is why Nabil's Image Books exist. Images can convey a lot of information; whether they are simple artwork or engineering graphs and models. The Image Books that are in stock show the colors lavender and green, and everything in between.
Audio Books
To help pass time, whether it's waiting for an appointment or commuting to work, one can learn new information and become more knowledgeable through listening to an Audio Book. Nabil's Audio Books are there to make the most out of otherwise wasted time. One can play an Audio Book in the background, and any mindless task suddenly becomes a very productive one.
Video Books
Video Books are the next type of media shared by the bookstore. Video Books are a mixture of Image Books (frames) and Audio Books (an audio track). Getting the best of both worlds comes at a storage cost however, as video books range in sizes from 45 MB to 119 MB. This high cost is examined more thoroughly in the experiment, as the founder considers purchasing a separate disk to serve Video Books.
Miscellaneous Books
Wildcard books that are not in any of the previous categories are considered Miscellaneous Books. The Miscellaneous Books that are currently in stock are .zip, .pptx, and .pdf files. Customers may download and analyze Zip Books to learn about file compression of different media, or they may go over the slides in Powerpoint Books to learn about virtual memory and caching. Either way, knowledge is always there.

The Experiment
From their descriptions, and due to the fact that they are all free, one can see why demand for Nabil's Books is so high. The high demand however causes the server to clog up and possibly crash when too many requests are being processed concurrently. Customers sometimes wait 30 seconds from the server before their Book download starts. The problem was seen by management, and it needed to be fixed. Should more CPUs be installed or should another disk be used to serve Video Books? This is what the research paper explores.
