Nabil's Books

Introduction
Nabil's Books is an online bookstore that serves books to millions of knowledge seekers worldwide. The knowledge it offers comes in a variety of file formats and sizes, and a vast number of requests get processed concurrently by the bookstore. Over time, as its business model is to serve all types of knowledge for free, regardless of availability, Nabil's Books has gained enough popularity to become world renowned. Due to its popularity, however, consumer traffic began to cause longer than expected download speeds, and many customers left frustrated with the free service due to that. The founder of the bookstore, Nabil, wants to make his bookworms happy, so he launches an investigation to determine the root cause of these latencies.

The Bookstore
Knowledge comes in many forms, and many file formats. Nabil's Books is proud to offer books of all common media formats one comes across in the web. These are the types of books Nabil's Books has in stock:
Text Books
The original media type that Nabil's Books provides is Text Books. Nabil's Text Books contain a vast amount of data, all written in Latin. To exemplify how much knowledge Nabil's Text Books have, the smallest Text Book, 5MB.txt, has 757,760 words. The average reading speed is 200 words per minute, so it would take an average person 63 hours to read that book alone!
Image Books
Sometimes, knowledge can be captured through images instead of text, and that is why Nabil's Image Books exist. Images convey lot of information; whether they are art pieces, analytical charts, or engineering designs. The Image Books that are in stock show two colors, lavender and green. However, the images are so detailed that virtually every color in between is captured through the pixels within.
Audio Books
To help pass time, whether it's waiting for a doctor to show up or commuting to work, one can learn new information and become more informed through listening to an Audio Book. Nabil's Audio Books are there to make the most out of otherwise wasted time. One can play an Audio Book in the background, and any mindless task suddenly becomes highly productive. The Audio Books that are available contain cryptic sounds left by aliens.
Video Books
Video Books are the next type of media shared by the bookstore. Video Books are a mixture of Image Books (frames) and Audio Books (audio tracks). Getting the best of both worlds comes at a storage cost however, as video books range in sizes from 45 MB to 119 MB. This high cost is examined more thoroughly in the experiment, as the founder considers purchasing a separate disk to serve Video Books. The Video Books that are available show an alien and his natural lifestyle, working at 3 AM to finish this project.
Miscellaneous Books
Wildcard books that are not in any of the previous categories are considered Miscellaneous Books. These book formats are very useful for knowledge transfer and presentation. The Miscellaneous Books that are currently in stock are .zip, .pptx, and .pdf files. Customers may download and analyze Zip Books to learn about file compression of different media, or they may go over the slides in Powerpoint Books to learn about virtual memory and caching, a topic explored in CS 367.

The Experiment
From their descriptions, and due to the fact that they are all free, one can see why demand for Nabil's Books is so high. The high demand, however, causes the server to clog up and crash when traffic is too high. Customers sometimes may wait 30 seconds from the server before their Book download starts when 1,000 people are downloading concurrently. The problem was seen by management, and it had to be fixed. Should more CPUs be installed or should another disk be used to serve Video Books? The paper explores this very question, and more!
