Nabil's Books

Introduction
Nabil's Books is an online bookstore that serves books to millions of knoweledge seekers worldwide. The knowledge it serves comes in a variety of file formats and sizes, and a vast number of requests get processed concurrently by the bookstore. Over time, as its business model is to serve all types of knowledge for free regardless of location, Nabil's Books has gained enough popularity to become an internationally recognized monopoly. Due to its popularity, however, consumer traffic has started causing longer than expected download speeds, and many customers leave unsatisfied with the free service due to that. The founder of the bookstore, Nabil, wants to keep his customers happy, so he launches an investigation to find out the root cause of these latencies and how to address them.

Text Books
The original media type that Nabil's Books serves is Text Books. Nabil's Text Books contain a vast mountain of information, all written in Latin. To exemplify how much knowledge Nabil's Text Books have, the smallest Text Book, 5MB.txt, has 757,760 words. The average reading speed is 200 words per minute, so it would take 63 hours to read that book alone.
